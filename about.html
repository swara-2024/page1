About Us

Mission

Our mission at Swara is simple: we want every child to speak clearly and confidently. No matter where they live, we believe all kids should have the chance to express themselves well. That's why we offer our platform for free. Our goal is to help children in cities and villages alike improve their speech skills. We're here to support them as they grow, making sure they can communicate with ease.

Vision

At Swara, we imagine a world where learning to speak better is fun for kids. By adding games to our platform, we're changing how speech therapy works. Our vision is to create a fun place where kids want to learn. We want them to enjoy getting better at speaking. With our technology and new ideas, we're making speech therapy exciting and rewarding.

Achievements

Swara is proud to be recognized for doing great work in education. We were chosen for the Aavishkar competition in 2023-24. Out of 35,000 entries, 150 students using our platform made it to the competition. This shows we're making a real difference. We're thrilled to be honored for our efforts. And we're committed to helping even more children around the world.
